This project examines the existing state of T-Mobile cellular coverage in Los Angeles County as well as three possible scenarios for increasing coverage. Scenario A is an increase of all existing tower heights by 10 meters. Scenario B increases the transmission radius of each tower from 25 to 30 kilometers. Finally, Scenario C adds three towers within the county at locations selected during the study.
