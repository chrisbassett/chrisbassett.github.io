Campground Site Suitability Analysis

This project presents the results of a site suitability study for a summer campground located in the Laguna and Cuyamaca Mountains of San Diego County. This study scored locations in the study area on five criteria: elevation, slope, proximity to major roads, vegetation type, and proximity to existing recreational trails. This analysis did not examine property ownership or existing land use, both of which should be incorporated into the next phase of study as those are likely to be limiting factors for potential development.
