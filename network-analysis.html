This project examined the market areas within a three minute drive of existing Starbucks locations in Riverside, California, using network analysis to evaluate catchment areas within a three minute drive of each location. The outcome was to determine which of the locations should be recommended for closure based on given criteria and analysis of each location's market area.

The supplied decision-making criteria was market area population, with the locations servicing the three smallest market areas to be subjected to further examination of demographic and other characteristics to produce a single recommendation. Demographic data considered were median income, median age, and percentage of population having attained a four-year college degree or higher educational level, calculated based on ACS 2019 block group data for all locations.
