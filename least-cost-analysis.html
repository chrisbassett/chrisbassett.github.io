Darién Gap Least-cost Route Analysis

Two options for bridging the Darién Gap on the Panama-Colombia border were examined through cost path analysis. Both routes originate in Yaviza, Panama, with one option ending in Apartadó, Colombia and the other in Chigorodó, Colombia. As this was a preliminary analysis, slope and the presence of water features were used to generate the cost surface used.
